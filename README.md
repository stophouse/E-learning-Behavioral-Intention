Data Availability Statement

The original dataset containing survey responses from 966 students was provided by Dr. Tomasz Szopiński and remains his property. For this reason, I am not authorized to publicly share the raw data.

In this repository, I include only the headers of the original dataset to illustrate its structure. These do not contain any sensitive or proprietary information.

Researchers interested in accessing the full dataset are encouraged to contact Dr. Tomasz Szopiński directly. As of March 2026, he is affiliated with Vizja University.
