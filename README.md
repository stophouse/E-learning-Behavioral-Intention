1. This project contains extra files to the paper 
'Perceived Usefulness Thresholds and Barrier Asymmetry in Students’ Behavioral Intention toward E-Learning: An Explainable Machine-Learning Analysis in Post-Pandemic Higher Education'

2. Data Availability Statement
The original dataset containing survey responses from 966 students was provided by Dr. Tomasz Szopiński and remains his property. For this reason, I am not authorized to publicly share the full raw data.
In this repository, I include only:
- the headers and the first 5 rows of the original dataset, and
- the headers and the first 5 rows of the processed dataset used for machine learning modelling
These excerpts are provided solely to illustrate the data structure and do not contain sensitive or proprietary information.
Researchers interested in accessing the full dataset are encouraged to contact Dr. Tomasz Szopiński directly. As of March 2026, he is affiliated with Vizja University.
